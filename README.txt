Installation Instructions

1.  Run Cura at least once.
   -  If you have never opened Cura:
     a. Open Cura
     b. Click “I understand and agree”
     c. Close the “Add Printer” wizard
     d. Close/Exit Cura
2.  Unzip the downloaded file to a destination of your choosing
3.  With Cura closed, copy the definitions, meshes, and quality folders into your user settings folder.
   -  Windows: %USERPROFILE%\AppData\Roaming\cura\3.x
   -  macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/cura/3.x
   -  Linux: ~/.local/share/cura/3.x
4.  Open Cura
5.  From the “Add Printer” wizard click “Other” then scroll down until you find “Monoprice Mini Delta”
